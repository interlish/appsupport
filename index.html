Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Open the app and explore our episode library. Choose any topic that interests you and start listening. Your 3-day free trial begins automatically.

What's included in Premium?

Premium unlocks all episodes, background playback, offline downloads, and unlimited dictation practice. New content is added weekly.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Your Name ‚Üí Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Interlish, and tap Cancel Subscription.

Can I use Interlish offline?

Yes. Download episodes while connected to Wi-Fi and enjoy them anywhere, anytime.

Is my progress synced across devices?

Your learning progress is automatically synced when you sign in with the same account on multiple devices.
